The login step asks you to login into ProteomeXchange before submitting your dataset, this will also help us to identify your submission in the future.

Simply type in your PRIDE user name and password, then click on the "Login" button. Upon success, it will take you to the next step of the submission. Error message will be shown as popup if the login has failed.

If you are not registered, please click on the "Register" link below the "Login" button.
